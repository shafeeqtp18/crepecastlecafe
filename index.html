Welcome To Crepe Castle Cafe

Little Story

Crepe Castle Cafe is a restaurant that sells delicious crepes and desserts. It is located in Dubai exactly in the Jumeirah Village Circle. We also serve coffee, fresh juices, milkshakes and mojitos. Visit the menu page to learn more about the menu.

Delivery is within 15-20 km to all destinations, including Sports City, Arjan and Al Barsha. You can order food through the food delivery app like Talabat, Deliveroo, Careem, Zomato and Smiles. We are open to you every day from 9am to 3am Launched in 2021, this company will always be open to you as a Crave Place.
